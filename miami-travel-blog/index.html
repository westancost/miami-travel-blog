Featured Miami Travel Guides

Miami Travel Essentials: What to Pack for Sunny Weather

Choosing the right fabrics, such as linen and cotton, will keep you cool during humid afternoons. You should also prioritize high-SPF sunscreen and a wide-brimmed hat to protect against intense tropical UV rays. Do not forget to include a versatile jacket or sweater for heavily air-conditioned indoor spaces. Finally, packing a waterproof wet bag ensures your damp swimwear stays separate from your dry items.

Best Beaches in Miami for First-Time Visitors

Discover beginner-friendly Miami beaches for relaxing, swimming, walking, and enjoying Florida sunshine. These top-rated spots feature calm, shallow waters that are ideal for nervous swimmers and young children. You will also find plenty of convenient amenities, such as clean restrooms, outdoor showers, and shaded picnic areas. Dedicated lifeguards regularly patrol these shorelines to provide an extra layer of safety and peace of mind. Accessible parking lots located just steps from the sand make arriving and unpacking completely stress-free.
